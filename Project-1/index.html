STREE 2

"Stree 2" is the sequel to the 2018 hit horror-comedy "Stree," directed by Amar Kaushik and produced by Dinesh Vijan under Maddock Films. The film will see the return of its original cast, including Rajkummar Rao, Shraddha Kapoor, Pankaj Tripathi, Aparshakti Khurana, and Abhishek Banerjee. While plot details are still under wraps, "Stree 2" is expected to pick up from where the first movie left off, further exploring the supernatural elements and continuing the blend of humor and horror. The film is anticipated to release in late 2024.

DANGAL

"Dangal" is a 2016 Indian biographical sports drama directed by Nitesh Tiwari and starring Aamir Khan as Mahavir Singh Phogat, a former wrestler who trains his daughters, Geeta and Babita Phogat, to become world-class wrestlers. Based on a true story, the film explores themes of gender equality and empowerment as it follows the Phogat family's journey in the male-dominated sport of wrestling. "Dangal" was widely acclaimed for its storytelling and performances, particularly by Aamir Khan and the lead actresses, and became a massive box office success, setting records as one of the highest-grossing Indian films worldwide.
